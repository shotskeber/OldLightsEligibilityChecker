About Old Lights

The Old Lights emblem will be awarded to any player whose account recorded any amount of playtime in each of Destiny 2's expansion years.

Once eligible, you will receive:
Old Lights Emblem
Elemental Qi Emote

Rewards are granted in waves. Keep an eye on Bungie.net for the latest updates.
